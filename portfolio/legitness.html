Technical Overview

Legitness is a native iOS marketplace designed to address authenticity concerns in the premium second-hand clothing market. Buyers often struggle to verify whether luxury streetwear and designer items are genuine when purchasing from resale platforms. The app combines a peer-to-peer marketplace with a layered authentication system to reduce counterfeit sales and build trust.

Core Features

  • Peer-to-Peer Marketplace – List and purchase premium clothing with structured product pages and high-resolution imagery
  • Community Authentication – Members review products, share authenticity opinions, and flag suspicious listings
  • Platform Verification – In-house checks for originality and quality to complement community input
  • Trust & Transparency – Verified badges, authenticity scores, and clear provenance to help buyers purchase confidently

Architecture & Implementation

Developed with Swift and UIKit using an MVVM architecture. The app integrates with backend services for listings, user profiles, authentication workflows, and moderation. Image upload and verification flows support both community and platform-level checks.

Tech Stack

Swift, UIKit, Core Data, REST APIs, image processing, modular architecture, secure authentication
