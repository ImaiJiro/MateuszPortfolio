Project Overview

The Mom-me-Downs App is designed as a marketplace for parents to buy, sell, and swap gently used kids' items (clothing, toys, gear) with other parents. The value proposition: rather than brand-new expensive items and chain stores, this app supports the "hand-me-down" culture where parents can pass things on, making kid-item reuse easy and local.

Key Features

  • Upload and list items – Take photos and list items to reach other parents in the area
  • Buy items – Browse local listings of gently used kids' items
  • Swap/trade items – Trade items with other users rather than purely buying and selling
  • Location-based marketplace – Geared toward local reach for convenient exchange or delivery
  • Messaging / Inbox – In-app messaging between users

Tech Stack

Swift, UIKit, CoreLocation, REST APIs, Core Data, MVVM
