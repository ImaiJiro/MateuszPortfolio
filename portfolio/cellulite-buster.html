Project Overview

A research-backed workout program built for women who want to reduce cellulite. Focuses on targeted exercises for cellulite reduction and toning of legs and buttocks. Users can do focused workouts at home without equipment.

Key Features

  • 15 most effective anti-cellulite exercises targeting thighs, legs, and buttocks
  • No equipment required – exercises done at home
  • Clear instructions and video support for each exercise
  • Timers and workout tracking with calendar/graph view
  • Top 10 Nutrition & Lifestyle Tips
  • Reminder notifications for workouts
  • Offline capability – all content included

Tech Stack

Swift, UIKit, Core Data, AVPlayer (video), local notifications
