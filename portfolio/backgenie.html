BackGenie – Back Pain Relief

Health and fitness mobile app designed to help users relieve and prevent back pain through a structured 6-minute exercise program. Physiotherapist-approved routines with workout tracking and progress monitoring.

Platform:
  • iOS
  • Swift
  • UIKit
Focus:

Health · Fitness · Timer

Tech:

Swift · Core Data · Timers

Back

Project Overview

BackGenie features exercises chosen by leading physiotherapists, providing users with an efficient and effective way to maintain a pain-free back. The application includes workout tracking, progress monitoring, and detailed exercise instructions to guide users through each routine.

Key Features

  • 6-minute structured workout program for back pain relief
  • Exercise library with detailed instructions and demonstrations
  • Progress tracking and workout completion statistics
  • Exercise timer with countdown and completion indicators
  • Monthly exercise tracking and history
  • Social sharing capabilities for workout achievements
  • Physiotherapist-approved routines (knee rolls, single knee to chest stretch, etc.)

Tech Stack

Swift, UIKit, Core Data, AVFoundation (timers), local notifications
